ENG

Marvin Mayard is a freelance designer based in Paris (FR). He works on web projects, brand identities, publishing and video production, with a particular interest in digital, interactivity, architecture, photography and contemporary art. He is the co-founder and creative director of the Culturé collective. He also produces live VJ-ing performances. Available to help you develop your image, your digital or print project, or just to collaborate   See you soon ︎!
